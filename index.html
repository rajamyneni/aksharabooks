Welcome to Akshara


We are a neighborhood sanctuary for stories.

Akshara Books has been a quiet home for Hyderabad's readers since 1997. We believe that bookstores are not just about selling books; they are about the pause in a busy day, the smell of paper, and the discovery of a world you didn't know existed.

We are thoughtful about what we put on our shelves. Whether you are looking for a bedtime story for your child or a classic novel in Telugu, our collection is curated for the curious mind.
