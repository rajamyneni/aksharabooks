Our Story

Three Decades of
Literary Passion

Founded in 1997 by Ashok and Lakshmi, Akshara Books began as a small corner shop in Begumpet with just 500 carefully selected titles. The name 'Akshara' means 'letter' or 'syllable,' reflecting our belief that every word carries the power to transform minds.

What started as a labor of love has grown into one of Hyderabad's most cherished literary institutions. Our shelves have witnessed countless first discoveries, hosted legendary authors, and created a community bound by the love of the written word.

Today, we curate over 50,000 titles across our carefully organized collections, from rare first editions to contemporary bestsellers, always maintaining the intimate atmosphere of a private library.

Our Journey

A Legacy in Letters

1997

The Beginning

Akshara Books opens its doors in Begumpet with 500 hand-picked titles. A small lamp, a few chairs, and endless dreams.

2003

First Expansion

Moved to our current heritage building in Banjara Hills. Added our beloved reading room and café corner.

2010

Literary Hub

Launched our Author Talk series. Hosted literary giants including Ruskin Bond, Amitav Ghosh, and contemporary voices.

2017

Two Decades

Celebrated 20 years with the launch of our Rare Books collection and preservation program for Telugu literature.

Today

The Journey Continues

Over 50,000 titles, a thriving community of readers, and the same passion that started it all. The best chapters are yet to be written.
